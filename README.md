# Microstructure Classification Workshop

Notebook for exploring some methods for image classification, focussing on visual bag of words (VBOW) and pretrained
convolutional neural networks (CNNs).

Click below to open in Google Colab.
Once there, open the tutorial.ipynb and **make a copy** of the notebook to your Google Drive.
You can then run the cells at the top to mount your Google Drive and download the data we
will be using.
